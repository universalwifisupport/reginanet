Our Story

Independent Service Guidance

Regina Net is an independent third-party service assistance provider founded in 2025. We are not an ISP and are not affiliated with any cable, internet, phone, or streaming provider.

The connectivity services landscape is complex — filled with industry jargon, bundled packages, and sales-driven messaging. We created Regina Net as an independent resource where customers can turn for unbiased informational support and guidance from a team with no provider affiliation or agenda.

Our role is limited to providing guidance and informational assistance only. We do not activate services, sell plans, or provide official support for any brand. We help customers understand their options and approach service decisions with clarity.
